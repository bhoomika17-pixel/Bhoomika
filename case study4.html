Research Insights

visibility

Recognition vs. Noise

Quantitative analysis (ANOVA) proved that increased vertical offset significantly improves character recognition for dyslexic and low-vision users by reducing visual interference.

biotech

Anchor Point Efficiency

Identified that stakeholder concerns regarding "visual heaviness" were not supported by data; instead, the adjustments facilitated more efficient anchor-point identification during rapid scanning.

groups

Universal Performance

Demonstrated that refinements for specific accessibility needs improved visual comfort and readability for the general user base, confirming no usability trade-offs were required.
